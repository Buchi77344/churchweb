ABOUT US

Brief History: This church started her first Sunday fellowship on 23rd, May 2004, it was formally commissioned by my spiritual father APOSTLE DR ENOCH OLANIYI ILEMOBAYO the founder of WORLD CHANGER CHURCH,from Lagos In October 2014. The church became incorporated February 2019, we thank God for what we have been through and what God is doing now and what He will be doing in Continuation.

Vision Statement: Delivering human souls from the sick bed of destruction by teaching and applying the power in the finished work of Christ.

Mission Statement: Reaching out to the whole world through the message of Grace and Hope,and causing people to understand God's endless love and the Reality of His Kingdom.
